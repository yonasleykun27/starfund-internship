EthioFarm Connect

We are building an AI-powered supply chain platform that connects smallholder farmers in rural Ethiopia directly to urban markets, cutting out middlemen and increasing farmer income by up to 40%. Our platform uses SMS-based technology to reach farmers with no smartphones, and a mobile app for market buyers and logistics partners.

🎯 The Problem

Over 12 million Ethiopian smallholder farmers sell their produce at extremely low prices because they lack access to real-time market data and direct buyer connections. Up to 35% of produce is wasted due to poor logistics coordination. We are changing this by bringing the entire supply chain onto a single, accessible digital platform.

💡 Our Solution

📱

SMS Market Prices

Real-time market price alerts delivered via SMS to feature phone users, requiring no internet connection.

🚚

Logistics Network

Coordinated transport booking system connecting certified truck drivers to farmer cooperatives.

🤝

Direct Buyer Matching

AI matchmaking connects farmers directly to restaurants, hotels, and supermarkets in Addis Ababa.

💳

Mobile Payments

Instant payment via CBE Birr and Telebirr directly to the farmer's mobile wallet upon delivery confirmation.
